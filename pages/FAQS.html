FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

Welcome to EyeQ. Have a question? Check out our FAQ section for helpful answers! By using our app, you're agreeing to the terms mentioned here, so feel free to read through to make sure you're all set.

1. Who can use this eye test?

By accessing or using EyeQ, you agree to these Terms and our Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

2. How accurate are the results?

The results from EyeQ can provide a general idea of your eye health, but they are not a substitute for a professional eye exam. For a complete diagnosis, it's best to visit an eye care provider.

3. Is this test suitable for people with existing eye conditions?

While our eye test can detect common vision issues such as near-sightedness, far-sightedness, and colorblindness, individuals with more complex conditions should seek advice from an optometrist or ophthalmologist.

4. Do I need any special equipment to take the test?

No special equipment is required. All you need is a device with a screen (phone, tablet, or computer) and a stable internet connection.

5. Can children or people over 65 use the app?

Currently, the app is designed for users between the ages of 18 and 45. If you're under 18 or over 65, please consult a healthcare professional for more personalized eyecare.

6. How often should I take this test?

We recommend taking the test every 6-12 months to monitor your eye health. However, if you notice any significant changes in your vision, please consult an eye care professional immediately.

7. What should I do if I have difficulty completing the test?

If you have trouble reading the test or experience discomfort during it, stop immediately and consult an eye care provider for further guidance.

8. Are the test results private?

Yes, your privacy is important to us. All your test results are kept confidential and are not shared with third parties without your consent.
