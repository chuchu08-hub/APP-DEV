About us

At EyeQ, we offer online tests designed to help you assess whether you may have a vision deficiency. Our platform provides a set of interactive questions that can help you identify potential issues with your eyesight.

Please note, the results from the test are not a diagnosis, but a preliminary tool to help you determine if you should seek professional eye care. We recommend following up with an optometrist or healthcare provider for a full evaluation.
